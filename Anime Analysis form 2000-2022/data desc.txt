About Dataset
Content

This dataset is composed of more than 3400 anime series from 1990 to June 20, 2022. The data were obtained from the website animeflv.net. A well known website for anime series viewing.

About the columns and data

title-> Anime name
description-> Anime synopsis
year-> Release year
type-> Anime Category: Movie (Pelicula), OVA, Anime
rate_start-> Number of voting stars
votes-> Total number of votes
status-> Status of the series: On air (En emisiÃ³n), completed (Finalizado), coming soon (Proximamente)
followers-> Number of people following the series
episodes-> Number of episodes in the series
genders-> Genres to which the series belongs
url_anime-> URL of the official anime website
image-> URL of the anime image
